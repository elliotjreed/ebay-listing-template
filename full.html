Contact Me

The easiest way to contact me is using eBay's messaging ("Contact Seller") system. I normally have my phone with me, so should be able to get back to you pretty quickly.

Delivery, Returns & Refunds

Delivery

I send things out using either Royal Mail or an alternative courier for larger items. Royal Mail's "48" service normally gets things delivered within 2 days, but has been known to be a bit slower than that sometimes.

Royal Mail's "24" service is normally pretty good and gets things delivered the next day, but can sometimes be the day after. Other couriers are usually 1 to 2 days.

If your order does not arrive within 5 days for the Royal Mail "48" service, and 3 days for the Royal Mail "24" service, please let me know. With other courier please let me know if it's not with you within two days and I'll chase them up on your behalf.

Returns

For all orders it's fine of you want to return anything within 21 days, as long as it's in the original condition.

Refunds

Refunds will be refunded into the same method of payment used for the original purchase (ie. PayPal).
